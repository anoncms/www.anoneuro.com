Where to "invest", AnonEuro or AINT?

We can start with the word "invest". AnonEuro is basically equivalent to the bank account so you don't invest in it. You keep money on the AnonEuro account as savings and soon you'll be able to pay goods and services with it. On the other hand AINT is for investing. When you have money on AnonEuro account (own AnonEuros) you are AnonEuro user but when you own AINTs you are investor.

In reality it means that AnonEuro can be converted to EUR within 24 hours max and it has stable interest on savings. So largest risk is eventual interest rate drop.

When it comes to AINT token that means it might not be possible to sell AINT at the desired price if you want to sell it urgently or you'll have to sell it cheaper.On the other hand AINT token brings the real possibility of getting rich quick when AnonEuro (and other copies of it) get many users and investors. Also have in mind that just by holding AINT token you earn extra cryptocurrencies like automated and regular dividend payment in the world of stocks.

How AnonEuro keeps the value stable?

All Waves tokens that come to AnonEuro address are automatically converted to other crypto financial products within Waves ecosystem and those products have built-in interest on savings which varies but often goes over 25% per year. Read more about Waves here.

What is transaction price of AnonEuro?

Transaction price is EUR 0.05 (5 cents), regardless of the amount. Transactions can be payed with other tokens and cryptocurrencies from Waves system and with Waves itself. In that case transaction price is 0.001 Waves which is more expensive option at the moment.

Why are you anonymous?

There are several reasons why we are anonymous. The first is "security through obscurity" principle. While it is impossible to catch us all and get us to sign a transaction with our private keys all at once, we do not want anyone to even think about trying to rob us that way in person.

Another reason is that we believe that financial centers of power will not be content with our solution, although it will be fully decentralised and thus it will not make sense to prosecute individuals (same as in the context of robbery), we do not want to make it easier for the system to try and bully developers of AnonEuro and other similar solutions.

The third reason for anonymity is Satoshi Nakamoto, the inventor of Bitcoin, who is still anonymous today. Bitcoin was launched by a completely anonymous person, and the identity of that person is not at all important for the functioning of Bitcoin. This is one of the points of cryptocurrencies and the whole crypto world - anonymity and privacy without the need for trust. Everything we do and say we do is transparent and can be checked in the blockchain itself. Because we offer our customers anonymity, we require the same for ourselves. Moreover, we want AnonEuro as a quality software product to speak for itself.

The fourth and last reason is that the founders of Cryptokuna are members of the Anonymous group, for which anonymity and privacy along with freedom are the main ideals. In other words, we are also anonymous because it is fun.
