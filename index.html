Simple interest rate calculator

This is a simple interest rate calculator for an AnonEuro savings account. Enter an amount you will be saving in an AnonEuro account and the length of time, in years, that you plan to save. The calculator will show the amount you will have in total after so many years of saving and the monthly return on savings with current interest. You can calculate the amount in HRK, EUR or any other currency, the percentage stays the same regardless of the currency.

Amount in the account and number of years both need to be greater than 0.

* the amounts for daily and monthly earnings if the interest is to be paid immediately, and not added back to the AnonEuro account in order to obtain the effect of a compound interest account (interest on principal amount + accumulated interest).
